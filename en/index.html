About SP Healthcare Services

Our team, meanwhile 3 employees in the office and about 100 employees in the nationwide field service (scientists, pharmaceutical representatives, PTAs), already worked together successfully at PEIX Sales Plus and was integrated into the structures of S&P Healthcare Services at the end of 2019.

While the office staff plans the projects of the clients, the field staff visits the selected doctors, clinics and pharmacies throughout Germany or in individual federal states. Whether sample deliveries, studies or market research: Everything will be carried out and reported according to the agreement.

The field service works exclusively, that means they only discuss the client's product(s) with the doctor or clinic.

The "all-round service" extends from the organization of product training to the final report - also with a detailed project analysis if requested.
